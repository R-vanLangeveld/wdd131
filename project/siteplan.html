WDD 131 Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Ryndee's Game Reviews

The reason I chose this name is because I wanted a site to share my thoughts on some of the games I have played.

Purpose

A place for me to share tips & tricks for Xbox 1, PlayStations 4 & 5, Nintendo Switch, and PC games that I have played.
And receive suggestions for video games to review by visitors.

Audience

People looking for tips and tricks in video games

Scenarios

"Why haven't you played (non reviewed) game yet?" I don't own that game or it's one I haven't gotten around to playing yet.

"Can I sort through the games you've reviewed?" Yes, by price range and alphabetically.
