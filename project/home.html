About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is Ryndee and I like to play video games. I wanted a place to share my thoughts on the games I've played. So here is where you will find all my opinions on games I've played as well as what systems the games can be played on. Let me know if you have any game suggestions in the form at the bottom of the page.

Anticipated Questions

Why haven't you reviewed the game I requested?

Here are some reasons

  • I haven't gotten around to playing it
  • I'm not interested in it right now
  • I haven't seen the request
  • I don't have an account with that platform
  • I haven't played enough of it to make a good review
  • I'm busy with other things in life

Please be patient with me

What do you mean by "platform"?

Is the PC game on Steam, Epic games, or something I don't know about so it isn't listed here? Please type all the platforms the game is on when you request a game that can be played on a PC.

Will I receive credit if you review the game I requested?

If you were the first person to request it. However, if the first person to request the game doesn't put their name in the form, I will also give credit to the first person to put their name in the form.
Example of how it will look: "Requested by: Anonymous, name"

Are there spoilers on this site?

I will say if a game contains spoilers for another game, but I will not include any details about the plot of any game.
